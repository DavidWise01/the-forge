Geometry

Each cardinal station is a static heptad: six outer emitters fixed around one local core. The local core provides the dead-center carrier for that direction; the six satellites provide boundary pressure.

Timing

The sequence starts at zero and pushes outward by direction. Each direction owns one quarter-phase. The full compass completes after four gated emissions.

Containment

Energy is visualized as a local simulation only. The zero well receives pulses, collapses to 00, then reopens to 27 without cross-arm bleed.
