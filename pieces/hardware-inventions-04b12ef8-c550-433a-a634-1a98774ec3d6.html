📖 README - Wet Chip Design

Ar Gas Isolation Layer

  • Ar (3s² 3p⁶) has 0 unpaired electrons = 0 lasers
  • Fills capsid gaps between Ti3SiC2 cores
  • At 1 GPa: density increases 100×, becomes photon barrier
  • Prevents O1 diffusion and electrical shorts

Wet Chip Stack Logic

  • Ti3SiC2 stack: 3 Ti atom layers + 1 SiC atomic ribbon
  • Ar capsid surrounds each stack as inert insulator
  • Cu/Zn/Ag/Au provide conduction paths
  • 1 GPa pressure = diamond anvil cell equivalent

Operation

  • Build: Create 10×10×10 lattice with Ar gaps
  • Stack: Add Ti3SiC2 + Ar isolation layer
  • IN Pulse: Inject electron carriers
  • 10kHz: Toggle clock for D computation
  • O1: Oxygen test injection (blocked by Ar at 1GPa)
  • Pressure: Control Ar density 0-2 GPa

Physics Model

  • Leak = 100% at 0.1MPa, 0% at 1.0GPa+
  • D = d_toggled / total_cores per 100ms
  • OUT = D × conduction_factor × num_cores
  • Photon tunneling ∝ 1/Ar_density²
