Point 17 is the battery and the judge.

Fixed geometry

Four quadrants prevent drift. Four beams per quadrant create symmetric pressure. No emitter gets to chase the center; the center must reconcile all incoming color vectors.

Capacitance well

The white center is drawn as a storage field. Incoming beams increase charge and phase density. The brighter ring means stored energy, not arbitrary glow.

Gated release

When coherence rises, the well can dump a narrow white pulse. If coherence is low, the well holds, damps, and prevents color bleed across boundaries.
