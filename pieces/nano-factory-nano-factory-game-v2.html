Welcome to NanoFactory

Assemble molecules atom by atom. A calm, Factorio-inspired nanoscale puzzle.

  1. Mine H and O manually (left panel). Costs 1⚡ each.
  2. Place a Miner on a colored node, then Conveyors (R to rotate).
  3. Route atoms into an Assembler to craft Water (2H + 1O).
  4. Send output to Storage — it sells automatically for credits.
  5. Buy energy, research faster belts, and scale to Nanobots.
