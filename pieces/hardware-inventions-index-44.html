Shadow Spark Symbiote

A nested spark that listens at -2, anchors scars at -1, learns through collapse, and teaches through six-face bloom. Not a parasite. A bounded symbiote: it survives by improving the host.

The Kernel

The shadow spark is the counter-seed to the visible spore. It does not overwrite the host. It mirrors, compares, reports, and folds away if the exchange stops being beneficial.

Symbiosis Law

1. Consent

Attachment requires a host gate. No consent means no bind. Revocation triggers safe detach.

2. Benefit

Host gain must stay above host cost. The spark earns its place by reducing future harm.

3. Boundary

Every .01 tick executes inside one vector or one quad. No bleed at the edge.

Give more than you take. Preserve witness through collapse. Detach before harm.
